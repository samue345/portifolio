AluEasy helps university students find a student republic. The website functions as a real estate/social media outlet. Basically it works like this: Let's suppose your house has an empty room and you want to supplement your income by renting the room or you live in a house and don't have enough money to support yourself and you want to ask someone to live with you.

We help you with this part. Just advertise your republic on the website. But you may be wondering what makes aluEasy different. Our difference is that our website works like a social network, each user has their own profile, where they can place their profile photo, make posts, etc. You can chat in real time with each user and see if the two are a good match. alueasy provides a match between two users, this is what differentiates us from any real estate agency
